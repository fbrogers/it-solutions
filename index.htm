Solutions

Available apps, ready to be used by the campus community and accessible with minimal effort.

Below is a brief list of custom, purchased, and/or provided software web applications that either provide UCF faculty or staff solutions to common problems or provide a platform to quickly and easily build solutions without the need for technical expertise or interference.

Available Solutions

Event Attendance

Track and Report on Attendance

Event Attendance is the best way to take attendance at any size event. Users can create events and take attendance by either using a USB card swipe or typing in an attendee's card ISO number. Events can be restricted to only current students or open to any UCF Card holder. Event rosters can be exported for further reporting.

  • Perfect For: Replacing paper sign-in sheets; Logging interactions for a one-time event
  • How to Use: Contact Custom Solutions to request access

Student Card Swipe

Verify Students and Track Usage

First used by All Knight Study and the Knights Pantry, Student Card Swipe uses a student's UCF Card to both verify their status and log their usage for reporting. Once you have a USB card swipe, it couldn't be simpler: login, pick your location, and swipe away.

  • Perfect For: Verifying students are current; Tracking visits to a space or office
  • How to Use: Contact Custom Solutions to request access

My Web Applications

Quick Access to Authorized Web Apps

My Web Applications presents any campus member with a list of web applications verified to be accessible by their campus login (NID and NID password). To accomplish this, we maintain a list of restricted-access links and check logged-in users to determine access. The list is always growing and we welcome submissions, as long as access can be verified in Active Directory (AD).

  • Perfect For: Your browser's homepage; On-boarding employees
  • How to Use: Visit my.it.ucf.edu or contact Custom Solutions to inquire about adding additional software links

UCF Events

Post and Promote Campus Events

UCF Events is the online location for listing and promoting UCF events. The system makes it easy for you to create event listings and share events with the UCF community. Submitting an event into the events system requires a carefully constructed title, start date & time, category and location.

  • Perfect For: Display a calendar on your website; Share events with other organizations on campus; Recommend events for posting on UCF's Main Calendar
  • How to Use: Head over to UCF Events' Help Page to get started!

Building Your Own Solutions

Qualtrics Surveys

Survey software that can handle everything from simple questionnaires to detailed research projects. Design surveys with an intuitive drag-and-drop survey tool, powerful logic, 100 plus question types, and pre-built survey templates.

Dynamic Forms

Dynamic Forms provides a simple way for anyone to take any paper-based form and quickly build a sophisticated interactive, electronic form. Users can customize, design and publish professional-looking forms without the need for programming knowledge.

Microsoft Forms

Allows anyone to quickly and easily create custom quizzes, surveys, questionnaires, registrations and more. Add in Microsoft Power Automate, and you can transform a simple form into a powerful workflow.

Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is considered to be the "hub for teamwork" in Office 365. You can chat, call, and meet with members in your team, as well as share files and collaborate together.

This curated list is maintained by UCF IT Custom Solutions in an effort to spread awareness of available solutions. If you know of a custom UCF application and/or platform usable by all of campus that should be on this list, please let us know!
